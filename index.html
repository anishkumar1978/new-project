Kinkaku-ji, officially named Rokuon-ji (Deer Garden Temple), is a Zen Buddhist temple in Kyoto, Japan. It is one of the most popular buildings in Kyoto, attracting many visitors annually. It is designated as a National Special Historic Site, a National Special Landscape and is one of 17 locations making up the Historic Monuments of Ancient Kyoto which are World Heritage Sites

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I loved the movie a lot As I am a great fan of marvel Avengers: Endgame', which marks the end of the Infinity Saga, is spellbinding and surely an enthralling experience. The last film of the 'Avengers' franchise is remarkable and doesn't disappoint. Watching all our favorite superheroes in one film is just surpassing.



Frozen 2
Excellent film to see with kids

When people ask me what's my favorite Disney movie, I genuinely have no idea, but after watching Frozen 2 this week with my sisters, I can say with full confidence that my favorite Disney movie is Frozen 2. The first and foremost thing I love and admire is the love Anna show to Elsa, which is the best example of sister love. She always looks after her, checking on her, and do her best to protect her! I love how that is the center of the movie.
